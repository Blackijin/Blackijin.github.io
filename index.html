3 clicks.
That's it.

Do It is a Progressive Web App. Install it from your browser — no app store, no download page, no waiting. It runs like a native app and works fully offline.

1
Click Open App — it opens in Chrome or Edge.
2
Look for the install icon (⊕) in the right side of the address bar.
3
Click it and select Install. Done — it launches like any desktop app.
1
Click Open App to open in Firefox.
2
Click the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner.
3
Select "Install" or "Add to Home Screen".
1
Open the app on Chrome (Android) or Safari (iPhone).
2
Android: tap the install banner → "Add to Home Screen". iPhone: tap Share → "Add to Home Screen".
3
Tap Add. The icon appears on your home screen, works offline from now on.
