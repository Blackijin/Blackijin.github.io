Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for speed and simplicity, with just enough power to keep you organised.

Priority levels
Mark tasks High, Medium, or Low priority with a colour-coded flag. See what matters at a glance.
Due dates
Assign a due date to any task. Badges automatically show "Today", "Tomorrow", or days overdue.
Search
Instantly filter your tasks by keyword. Searches task names, notes, and subtasks as you type.
Drag to reorder
Grab any task and drag it to where it belongs. Works with mouse on desktop and touch on mobile.
Task notes
Expand any task to add rich notes — context, links, details — saved automatically as you type.
Daily streak
Build a completion streak by finishing at least one task each day. A badge tracks your momentum.
Recurring tasks
Set tasks to repeat on chosen days of the week. They appear automatically every morning.
Focus timer
Start a countdown timer on any task to work in focused sprints. See time remaining at a glance.
Offline-first
Works with no internet connection. Data is saved privately on your device — no cloud, no account.

Install

Install in 3 clicks.
No app store needed.

Do It is a Progressive Web App (PWA) — install it directly from your browser and it works like a native app.

  1. 1
    Click Open App — it opens in Chrome or Edge.
  2. 2
    Look for the install icon (⊕ or download arrow) in the address bar on the right-hand side.
  3. 3
    Click it and select "Install". Do It opens in its own window, just like a desktop app.
  1. 1
    Click Open App to open in Firefox.
  2. 2
    Click the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner.
  3. 3
    Select "Install" or "Add to Home Screen". Firefox will create a desktop shortcut.
  1. 1
    Open the app link on your phone in Chrome (Android) or Safari (iPhone).
  2. 2
    Android: Tap the install banner or menu → "Add to Home Screen".
    iPhone: Tap the Share icon → "Add to Home Screen".
  3. 3
    Tap Add. The app appears on your home screen and works fully offline.
